Why Early Elf Tickets Work

Premium citizens get exclusive access to Early Elf Tickets days before the general public sale opens. These are the cheapest tickets of the year. The perceived value is enormous — and it directly ties the €25/year subscription to real, measurable savings on every festival visit. One ticket discount pays for three years of membership.

Merch as Story Extension

Merchandise that sells is merchandise that belongs to the world. Every item for sale — online or at the festival — must have a lore reason to exist.

Kevin · Merchants

Only merchants whose products fit the world of Planet Eve. Kevin's brief: every stall should feel like it could exist in the kingdom. If it could be sold at an Ikea, it doesn't belong here.

Ahmad · Innkeeper

Manuel · Kyara · Deco

Charlotte · Deco

◆

Social Media — Less Work. Same Reach.

The rule: social media repurposes content that already exists. It never creates new content from scratch. AI accelerates this dramatically.

AI Tool

Content Repurposing

Every manga chapter → 3 social posts. Every Herald → 1 Instagram story. Every festival moment → short Reels clip. Tools: CapCut AI (Reels), Canva AI (statics), Claude (captions). One piece of content becomes five without extra creative work.

AI Tool

Scheduling & Posting

Tools: Buffer or Later for scheduling. Batch one week of posts in one session. The marketing team does not need to be on social daily — they need to be in the kingdom weekly, setting the rhythm.

Rule

Every Post Has One Job

Every social post ends with one action: visit elfia.com. Not "follow us." Not "like this." The link in bio goes to the citizenship registration page — not the festival homepage. Social is a funnel; the website is the kingdom.

Earn the digital passport AND a passport holder at least 5 times each → Amalin dispatches the physical passport by post. Free to earn; €3.95 shipping or free festival pickup.

Physical passport collected at Tourist Info Booth or delivered. Stamped at each embassy, entrance, and selected vendors. Every stamp is a memory of the kingdom.

NFC Upgrade — Coming 2027
Physical passport upgraded with embedded NFC chip. Tap at festival stations to sync physical stamps with the digital wallet. The two worlds fully merge. Luso builds; Amalin dispatches.
Amalin owns passport dispatch

When a citizen qualifies for a physical passport (digital passport + holder both earned 5×), the system flags them. Amalin reviews the queue, confirms the address, and dispatches via post. Maximum 500 shipments per month (EasyParcel → transitioning to NL fulfilment). This is a human-checked process — no automation on physical dispatch until volume demands it.

06

The Story.
The engine behind everything.

The manga is not content. The manga is the reason to become a citizen. Every chapter is a reason to return.

📖
Darja + Stefan · Live Now

Interactive Manga — Drach-oc

Chapters release every Wednesday at 18:00 CET. The interactive layer allows readers to make choices inside the story. Each chapter ends on a rehook — a question that can only be answered by returning next week. Free citizens read all chapters. Paid citizens get early access.

  • Stefan: script + Dopamine Structure per chapter
  • Darja: art + panel composition + card designs
  • Luso: interactive layer on elfia.com
🎬
Darja + Luso · 2026–2027

AI Anime — Little Drach-oc

A short AI-animated series set in the Drach-oc universe. For the €10/year Knight tier. Made with Kling AI (primary animation), Runway (scene extension), and ElevenLabs (voice). Darja as art director. Luso handles pipeline and delivery. Stefan writes the scripts.

  • Goal: 6 episodes, ~3–5 min each
  • Always ends with a call to visit elfia.com
  • Citizenship registration is the paywall
Future: Build Your Own Elfia Castle

A digital kingdom-building game where citizens construct their own corner of Planet Eve. Planned for 2028+. Every in-game object is an artefact from the manga world. Citizens who have earned more blessings and stamps unlock more building materials. The game deepens citizenship without requiring festival attendance — making Elfia a 365-day world.
